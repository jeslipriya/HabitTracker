Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025


GoalTracker is a client-side application designed to respect user privacy. We do not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.


1. Data Storage

All data entered into GoalTracker - including goals, progress, profile information, and settings - is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.


2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal information, analytics data, or usage metrics. Your information never leaves your device.


3. Third-Party Libraries

GoalTracker uses third-party libraries such as Chart.js for visualization. These libraries operate entirely within your browser and do not transmit data.


4. Data Export & Backup

When you export or import data, all operations occur locally on your device. You retain full ownership and responsibility for your backups.


5. Data Removal

You may delete all stored data at any time by clearing your browser’s local storage for this application.


6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect improvements or changes to the application. Updates will be reflected on this page.

This project is intended for educational and personal productivity use. No warranties or guarantees are provided.
