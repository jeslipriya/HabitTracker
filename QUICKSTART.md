### Step 3: Track Your First Day
1. Find your goal card on the dashboard
2. Click the **"M"** (Monday) button to mark today as complete

### Step 5: View Your Progress
1. Check the header stats at the top (Success Rate, Active Goals, Streak)
2. Scroll down to see each goal's individual progress
3. Mark more days complete as you progress through the week
